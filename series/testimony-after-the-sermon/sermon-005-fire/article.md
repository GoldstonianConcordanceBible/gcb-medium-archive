# After Sermon 005: Fire

## What God Showed Me After I Preached

Opening Scripture:

"He will baptize you with the Holy Spirit and fire." — Matthew 3:11

---

## What I Was Trying to Say

This sermon was about the fire.

Fire is not where I perform for God.

Fire is where God refines what He has already exposed and cleansed.

I was trying to say that I cannot get to fire without completing the mirror and the water.

If I skip the mirror, I avoid truth.

If I skip the water, I avoid cleansing.

If I rush to fire, I may confuse passion with formation.

Fire is not hype.

Fire is refinement.

---

## What I Actually Lived While Preaching It

While I was preaching, I realized fire is where obedience becomes public.

It is one thing to say I love God.

It is another thing to love people when I am tired, misunderstood, corrected, or stretched.

It is one thing to preach the Word.

It is another thing to obey the Word after the camera is off.

The fire showed me that the sermon does not end when I stop recording.

That is where the real test begins.

---

## What I Got Right

I believe I got the warning right.

You cannot enter fire with an unexamined heart.

You cannot enter fire without cleansing.

Fire without mirror becomes pride.

Fire without water becomes anger.

Fire with surrender, abiding, mirror, and water becomes refinement.

That is the difference.

---

## What I Should Have Corrected

I should have made clearer that fire is not punishment for those in Christ.

Fire is preparation.

Fire is God refining the vessel so the witness can be trusted.

I also should have slowed down and explained that fire requires love.

If I endure fire without love, I become hard.

If I endure fire with Christ, I become refined.

---

## What I Prayed About Afterward

Lord,

Do not let me chase fire before I have submitted to the mirror and the water.

Refine me without letting me become proud.

Strengthen me without letting me become harsh.

Give me courage without taking away tenderness.

Let the fire purify my witness.

Let the fire burn away what cannot remain.

Let me walk through it with You.

In Jesus’ name,

Amen.

---

## What God Is Teaching Me Now

God is teaching me that fire is the final public test of the formation flow.

Surrender gives God permission to take all of me.

Abiding keeps me connected to Christ.

Mirror exposes what needs correction.

Water cleanses what was exposed.

Fire refines what remains.

This is why I cannot rush.

The fire is holy when the order is honored.

---

## The Fifth Gospel Reflection

The Fifth Gospel is not only written in peaceful moments.

It is written in fire.

It is written when I obey under pressure.

It is written when I love when it is hard.

It is written when I keep preaching, teaching, praying, and spreading the Word because God called me to do it.

If people are reading my life, then the fire must show Christ, not ego.

---

## Closing Witness

I preached the sermon once.

Now I have to live it daily.
