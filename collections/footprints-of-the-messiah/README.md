# Series II — Footprints of the Messiah
## The Walk After Formation

This collection contains the active discipleship framework of the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) Medium Archive.

Series II is not formation.

Series II is execution.

After surrender, after Mirror → Water → Fire, and after abiding in Christ, the believer begins to walk.

---

## Series Thesis

The Gospel is not only something you believe.

It is something you become.

The Footprints of the Messiah represent the lived expression of faith—daily, visible, disciplined, and consistent.

This is the Fifth Gospel in motion.

---

## Canonical Sequence

1. Footprint I — Abide in Christ  
2. Footprint II — Obey the Word  
3. Footprint III — Destroy the Flesh  
4. Footprint IV — Walk in the Spirit  
5. Footprint V — Endure the Fire  
6. Footprint VI — Reflect Christ Publicly  
7. Footprint VII — Multiply the Witness  

---

## Execution Logic

Abide establishes connection.

Obey activates the Word.

Destroy the Flesh removes opposition.

Walk in the Spirit replaces the old nature.

Endure the Fire strengthens endurance.

Reflect Christ Publicly makes faith visible.

Multiply the Witness reproduces the life in others.

---

## Doctrine Layer

Mirror → Water → Fire

Series II operates on a life already formed through this doctrine.
