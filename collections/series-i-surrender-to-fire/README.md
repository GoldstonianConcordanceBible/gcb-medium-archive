# Series I — Surrender to Fire
## The Formation Before the Footprints

This collection contains the foundational formation sequence of the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) Medium Archive.

Series I is not the walking phase.

Series I is the formation phase.

Before the believer can walk the Footprints of the Messiah, the believer must first pass through surrender, truth, cleansing, refinement, and abiding.

---

## Series Thesis

The life of faith does not begin with performance.

It begins with surrender.

A person cannot walk as the Fifth Gospel while still ruled by the old self. Series I establishes the formation path that prepares the believer to live publicly, faithfully, and consistently in Christ.

---

## Canonical Sequence

1. Did You Have Salvation to Begin With?
2. Wrestling With God
3. God Is Good / Dios Es Bueno / Elohim Tov
4. Surrender
5. Mirror
6. Water
7. Fire
8. Abide in Christ

---

## Formation Logic

Surrender is the doorway.

Mirror reveals the truth.

Water cleanses and reforms.

Fire refines and tests.

Abide in Christ sustains the life that remains after formation.

---

## Doctrine Layer

Mirror → Water → Fire

Series I establishes the internal reformation necessary before the external walk.
