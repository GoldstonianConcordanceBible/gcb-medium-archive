# Footprint IV: Walk in the Spirit

## Opening Scripture

“Walk in the Spirit, and you shall not fulfill the lust of the flesh.” — Galatians 5:16

---

## Body

After the flesh is destroyed, the believer must learn how to walk.

Footprint IV is Walk in the Spirit.

This matters because the Christian life is not only about what you leave behind. It is about who now leads you forward.

If the flesh is destroyed but the Spirit is ignored, the believer becomes empty, dry, and vulnerable. The old self cannot simply be removed. The new life must be led by the Holy Spirit.

The Spirit teaches.

The Spirit convicts.

The Spirit comforts.

The Spirit reminds.

The Spirit empowers.

The Spirit guides the believer into obedience.

Walking in the Spirit means the believer stops being led by impulse, appetite, pride, fear, and old identity. The life is no longer ruled by reaction. It is ruled by God.

This is not vague spirituality.

This is daily submission to the Holy Spirit.

A Spirit-led life asks:

Lord, what are You teaching me?

Where are You correcting me?

Where are You restraining me?

Where are You sending me?

Where are You asking me to obey?

Footprint IV comes after Destroy the Flesh because the Spirit and the flesh are opposed. The believer cannot walk in both directions at the same time.

The flesh pulls backward.

The Spirit leads forward.

The flesh feeds appetite.

The Spirit forms holiness.

The flesh seeks applause.

The Spirit points to Christ.

The flesh protects self-rule.

The Spirit produces surrender.

To walk in the Spirit is to let God lead the life from the inside out.

This is where the Fifth Gospel becomes alive. A life led by the Spirit becomes readable because people begin to see fruit that cannot be manufactured by the flesh.

Love.

Joy.

Peace.

Patience.

Kindness.

Goodness.

Faithfulness.

Gentleness.

Self-control.

These are not decorations.

They are evidence.

The Spirit produces what performance cannot.

The Spirit forms what ambition cannot.

The Spirit sustains what emotion cannot.

The believer who walks in the Spirit does not merely avoid sin. The believer begins to carry the character of Christ.

This is the replacement after destruction.

Destroy the flesh removes the old ruler.

Walk in the Spirit receives the true Guide.

And this walk must be daily.

Not occasional.

Not only during sermons.

Not only during worship.

Not only when life feels holy.

Daily.

The Spirit must lead the private life, the public witness, the teaching, the repentance, the decisions, the reactions, and the mission.

This is where the disciple becomes sensitive to God.

The believer no longer asks only, “What can I get away with?”

The believer asks, “What honors Christ?”

The believer no longer asks, “How do I protect myself?”

The believer asks, “How do I obey the Spirit?”

The believer no longer asks, “How do I appear spiritual?”

The believer asks, “How do I remain faithful?”

Walking in the Spirit protects the believer from returning to flesh-power.

Because even ministry can become flesh if it is driven by ego.

Even content can become flesh if it is driven by attention.

Even theology can become flesh if it is driven by pride.

Even discipline can become flesh if it is separated from dependence on God.

That is why Footprint IV is essential.

The Spirit keeps the witness alive.

The Spirit keeps the archive submitted.

The Spirit keeps the teacher humble.

The Spirit keeps the public life connected to the private altar.

The Spirit keeps the Fifth Gospel from becoming performance.

Series I formed the believer through Surrender → Mirror → Water → Fire → Abide in Christ.

Series II now teaches the believer how to walk.

Abide in Christ established connection.

Obey the Word established direction.

Destroy the Flesh removed opposition.

Walk in the Spirit establishes daily leadership.

Now the believer is no longer asking the old self where to go.

The believer is being led by God.

And because the Spirit leads, the believer can endure what comes next.

Because after the Spirit leads the walk, the fire will test the walk.

That is the next Footprint.

Endure the Fire.
